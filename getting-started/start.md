# Start

## Getting Started with Hostvora

Before you can access our services, you will need to create a customer account. This section will guide you through the registration process step-by-step.

***

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit our billing portal: [**https://hostvora.com**](https://hostvora.com/)
2. Click on **“**[Create Account](https://hostvora.com/register)**”** in the navigation bar.
3. Fill in the required information:
   * Username
   * Email Address
   * Password
4. Submit the form to create your new Hostvora account.

***

### 2. Verify Your Email Address

After completing the registration form, you will receive a verification email from Hostvora.

1. Open your inbox and look for the message titled **“Verify Your Email”**.
2. Click the verification link inside the email.
3. Once your email is confirmed, your account will be fully activated.

***

### 3. You’re All Set!

After verifying your account, you can log in at any time by visiting:

[**https://hostvora.com**](https://hostvora.com/)

From here, you can manage your services, place new orders, view invoices, and more.
