# Host a server

## Hosting a Server on Hostvora

Once your Hostvora account is verified, you can deploy your first server. This guide walks you through the entire process, from placing an order to accessing your machine.

***

### 1. Choose Your Server Plan

1. Log in to the billing panel: [**https://billing.hostvora.com**](https://billing.hostvora.com/)
2. In the navigation menu, click on **“Servers”** → **“Create Server”**.
3. Browse through the available hosting products (e.g., Webserver, Voiceserver, game servers).
4. Select the plan that best fits your needs.
5. Click **“Create”**

***

### 2. Access Your Server

After deployment, you will find your server under:

**Server → Manage**

There you will see:

* Connection details
* All servers
* Control panel (start, stop, reinstall, console access)

***

### 5. Your Server Is Live

Once connected, you can begin installing software, deploying applications, or setting up your game server. If you ever need help, you can open a support ticket directly in the billing panel.
