# Your Account

#### How can I change my password? <a href="#wie-kann-ich-mein-passwort-andern" id="wie-kann-ich-mein-passwort-andern"></a>

**Forgot your password?**

If you have forgotten your password and are no longer logged in, you can click on "Forgot your password?" in the login area and have a new password sent to your registered email address.

***

**Change password (if logged in)**

To change your password, go to the "Profile Picture" in the top right corner of the Billing Panel, then go to "Profile" and you can change your password there.

Please note that the new password will also be changed in Pterodactyl (server web interface).

***

#### How can I change my email address? <a href="#wie-kann-ich-meine-email-adresse-andern" id="wie-kann-ich-meine-email-adresse-andern"></a>

You can change your email address via the billing site. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner and go to "Profile" to change your email address.

To change your email address, a link will be sent to both email addresses, which you will need to confirm. If you no longer have access to either email address, please open a support ticket.

***

#### How can I change my username? <a href="#wie-kann-ich-meinen-benutzernamen-andern" id="wie-kann-ich-meinen-benutzernamen-andern"></a>

To change your username, go to the "Profile Picture" in the top right corner of the Billing Panel, then go to "Profile" and you can change your username there.

***

#### How do I become a Hostvora partner? <a href="#wie-werde-ich-mc-host24-partner" id="wie-werde-ich-mc-host24-partner"></a>

To apply, please send your partner application via email to partners@hostvora.com or open a support ticket on the dashboard.

In general, we would like to support communities with 100 members or more. If you have fewer members in your community, please don't hesitate to contact us; we decide based on potential, not number.

***

#### What to do if your account has been hacked? <a href="#mein-account-wurde-gehackt-was-tun" id="mein-account-wurde-gehackt-was-tun"></a>

If your account has been hacked, there are several things you should do to regain full and secure access. Please contact our customer support so we can reverse any orders or payments you may have made. We are happy to assist you with this.

You should also change your password. Simply click "Forgot your password?" when logging in and have a new one sent to you. It's also important to change the password for your email address. Hackers often still have access to the email account and can therefore repeatedly gain unauthorized access to your user profile.
